Cancellation & Refund Policy

Clear and transparent policies to protect your booking

Policy Overview

At Deluxe Tours, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair and transparent, balancing the needs of our customers with the operational requirements of organizing world-class tours.

This policy applies to all tour packages, accommodations, and services booked through Deluxe Tours. Please read this policy carefully before making your booking.

Last Updated: October 4, 2025

Cancellation Timeline & Refunds

The refund amount depends on when you cancel your booking relative to the departure date:

Cancellation Period Refund Amount
More than 60 days before departure 90% refund (10% admin fee)
45-60 days before departure 75% refund
30-44 days before departure 50% refund
15-29 days before departure 25% refund
Less than 15 days before departure No refund
No-show or cancellation after departure No refund

Important Note

Cancellation must be requested in writing via email to info@deluxetour.co.ke or through your account dashboard. The cancellation date is determined by when we receive your written notice, not when you decide to cancel.

Booking Modifications

We understand that sometimes you need to adjust your plans rather than cancel entirely. Here's our policy for booking changes:

Date Changes

  • Changes requested more than 30 days before departure: $50 modification fee
  • Changes requested 15-30 days before departure: $150 modification fee
  • Changes requested less than 15 days before departure: Not permitted
  • Subject to availability on new dates

Tour Package Changes

  • Upgrades: Price difference + $50 processing fee
  • Downgrades: 50% of price difference refunded
  • Changes must be requested at least 30 days before departure

Traveler Name Changes

  • Minor spelling corrections: Free within 48 hours of booking
  • Complete name change: $100 fee + any airline penalties
  • Name changes must be completed at least 14 days before departure

Important

Some tour packages and services have special cancellation terms due to third-party suppliers. These will be clearly communicated at the time of booking and may be more restrictive than our standard policy.

Special Circumstances

Medical Emergencies

In case of serious illness or medical emergency preventing travel, we may offer:

  • Full refund minus non-recoverable costs (with valid medical certificate)
  • Credit for future travel valid for 12 months
  • Transfer to another person at no additional cost

Force Majeure

In cases of natural disasters, war, pandemic, government travel restrictions, or other events beyond our control:

  • We will work with you to reschedule your tour
  • If rescheduling is not possible, we will refund all recoverable costs
  • We are not liable for expenses incurred outside of the tour package

Tour Cancellation by Deluxe Tours

  • Full refund of all payments made
  • OR credit towards an alternative tour with 10% bonus value
  • We are not responsible for other expenses (flights booked separately, visa fees, etc.)

Refund Processing

How to Request a Cancellation

  1. Send an email to info@deluxetour.co.ke with your booking reference number
  2. Include the reason for cancellation and any supporting documents
  3. You will receive confirmation within 24-48 hours
  4. Our team will process your request and calculate the refund amount

Refund Timeline

  • Credit/Debit Card: 7-14 business days after approval
  • Bank Transfer: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Mobile Money: 3-5 business days after approval

Processing Time

Please allow 3-5 business days for us to review and approve your cancellation request. During peak seasons, this may take up to 7 business days.

Travel Insurance Recommendation

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to protect your investment. Travel insurance can cover:

  • Trip cancellation for any reason
  • Medical emergencies and evacuation
  • Lost or delayed baggage
  • Travel delays and interruptions
  • 24/7 emergency assistance

Travel insurance is not included in our tour packages but can be added during booking or purchased independently.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are generally non-refundable once booked:

  • Visa application fees
  • Travel insurance premiums
  • Third-party services booked on your behalf (certain activities, special events)
  • Custom tour arrangements and private services (depending on supplier terms)
  • Promotional or discounted packages (may have different terms)
